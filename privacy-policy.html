Privacy Policy

Panic Typing — Android App

Last updated: 31 March 2026

Overview

Panic Typing ("the app", "we", "us") is an Android word-typing game developed and published as an independent project. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

We are committed to handling your information responsibly. This app does not require an account, does not collect your name or email address, and does not store any personal data on our own servers.

Data We Collect

The app itself collects and stores only the following data, entirely on your device:

  • High scores — your best scores in Classic Mode and Time Trial are saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Panic Typing displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address and general location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. We do not have access to or control over the data AdMob collects. Google's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by adjusting your device settings:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • You can also reset your Advertising ID from the same menu

Score Sharing

The app includes an optional feature that allows you to share your score as an image card to social media platforms or messaging apps.

  • This feature uses your device's native share sheet — the app does not post anything on your behalf automatically.
  • The shared image is generated locally on your device and contains only your score and the app branding.
  • We do not transmit, store, or have access to any shared content.
  • Any data collected by the social media platform you share to is governed by that platform's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Panic Typing is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to address this.

Note: Google AdMob's advertising is configured in accordance with Google's policies on child-directed treatment. If you have concerns about advertising shown to children, please refer to Google's AdMob policies.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your high scores are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data persists until you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app's data via your device settings (Settings → Apps → Panic Typing → Clear Data)

We hold no data on our own servers, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service, which has its own privacy policy and data practices:

  • Google AdMob — advertising network. Google Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
